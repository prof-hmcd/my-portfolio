Who We Are

Engineered for the Fan Economy

Fangenics is a specialist sports marketing consultancy built at the intersection of data science, brand strategy, and fan culture. We partner with clubs, leagues, federations, and sporting brands to build meaningful, lasting connections with audiences around the world.

Our methodology is simple: understand the fan first. From grassroots community campaigns to global digital activations, every strategy we develop is grounded in deep audience intelligence and a relentless focus on measurable outcomes.

Whether you're looking to grow your fanbase, maximise commercial partnerships, or reshape your brand for a new generation — Fangenics delivers.
